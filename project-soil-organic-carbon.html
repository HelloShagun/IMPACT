Multimodal Remote Sensing and Learning for Soil Organic Carbon Monitoring

Remote Sensing & AI · Led by Marine Mercier

This project harnesses multi-source remote sensing data and AI to accurately predict and monitor soil organic carbon stocks.

The work spans classical machine learning approaches such as Random Forest and XGBoost, alongside deep learning with multimodal encoders including Convolutional Neural Networks and Long Short-Term Memory models. The aim is to enable scalable, cost-effective soil health assessment for climate modelling, agriculture, and carbon sequestration applications.
