Integrated Monitoring & Predictive Analytics for Cities & Tforwhat?

The IMPACT group develops satellite remote sensing and data-driven methods to assess, monitor, and improve the resilience of urban infrastructure — from bridges and tunnels to city-scale built assets.

Research Highlights

Key directions — full overview on Research
Core

Satellite Infrastructure Monitoring

Applying InSAR radar satellite imagery to detect millimetre-scale movements in bridges, tunnels, and urban assets — enabling large-scale structural health assessment from space.

Active

Data-Centric Asset Management

Combining sensor data, digital models, and machine learning to build predictive tools that help asset owners make informed maintenance and intervention decisions.

Featured

Disaster Risk & Urban Resilience

Fusing remote sensing data with computer vision and AI to assess structural damage, monitor landslide risk, and support early warning systems in vulnerable regions.
