MicroBlast: Disaster Risk Assessment from Space

EPSRC · Led by Wanli Ma

MicroBlast develops computer vision and minimal-supervision learning approaches to assess structural damage and monitor mass movement risk using satellite and aerial imagery in disaster-affected regions.

The project focuses on turning remote sensing data into operational information that can support rapid disaster response, exposure assessment, and infrastructure risk analysis.
