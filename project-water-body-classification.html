Geospatial AI for Water Body Classification

PhD Research · Led by Shagun Garg

This project explores geospatial AI methods for transferable water body classification across diverse environmental and urban contexts.

The work combines satellite data, feature engineering, foundation model embeddings, and leave-one-region-out validation to assess how well models generalise across regions and changing surface conditions.
